Shrinks and cracks under weather cycles
Fragile under seismic activity or marine environments
Steel
Extremely heavy (high transportation energy cost)
High geopolitical & price volatility
Thermal bridging (causes major building energy loss)

Together concrete & steel account for 15% of global emissions

Naturally Biophilic

Living and working within natural wood environments reduces human stress markers, improves cognitive fatigue, and raises mental wellness.

Easy to Fabricate & Machinable

Unlike steel and concrete, wood is highly machinable, versatile, and requires significantly less onsite curing or structural overhead.

Carbon Positive Sourcing

Wood physically locks carbon captured from the atmosphere, turning skyscrapers from carbon emitters into carbon sinks.
